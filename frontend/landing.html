About Us

Bridging the Information Gap in Rural Agriculture

FarmBuddy AI is developed with a single mission - to empower every farmer with the power of artificial intelligence

Type of Application

FarmBuddy is an AI-based agricultural advisory application. It is a multimodal and multilingual intelligent assistant for farmers. The application supports text, voice, and image-based inputs.

Objective

The main objective is to provide timely and accurate information to farmers. It helps farmers with crop advisory, market prices, and government schemes. The system aims to reduce the information gap in rural agriculture.

Methodology

The system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand farmer queries. Named Entity Recognition (NER) extracts crop, disease, and location details. A deep learning model is used for crop disease detection from images.

Datasets

The project uses publicly available agricultural datasets. These include government market price data and crop disease image datasets. Datasets are sourced from official portals, Kaggle, and Mendeley.
